  <h2>About the Game</h2>
<p>
CrossFire is a popular online first-person shooter (FPS) game developed by Smilegate.
In this game, players join two opposing teams called Global Risk and Black List.
Each team fights against each other in different missions and maps.
The main goal is to defeat the enemy team by using strategy, teamwork, and shooting skills.
</p>
<p>
The game offers many exciting modes such as Team Deathmatch, Search and Destroy,
and Free for All. Players can choose different weapons like rifles, pistols,
snipers, and grenades to defeat their opponents. Each weapon has different
strengths and weaknesses, so players must choose the right one depending on
their play style.
</p>

<p>
CrossFire is known for its fast-paced gameplay and competitive matches.
Many players enjoy the game because it allows them to work together with
friends, improve their reaction time, and experience intense battles in
different maps and environments.
</p>

<h2>Famous Guns in CrossFire</h2>


<h3><a href="https://crossfirefps.fandom.com/wiki/AK47"> AK - 47</a></h3>
<img src="https://d3075koojqalel.cloudfront.net/weapon/20161102094841_39014.png"width="350">
<p>The AK-47 is a powerful assault rifle known for its high damage. It is popular among skilled players who can control its strong recoil.</p>
<br>

<h3><a href="https://crossfirefps.fandom.com/wiki/M4A1"> M4A1</a></h3>
<img src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/crossfirefps/images/a/a1/Rifle_M4A1.png/revision/latest?cb=20160504161852"width="350">
<p>The M4A1 is a balanced rifle with good accuracy and stability. Many beginners and experienced players use it because it is easier to control.</p>

<h3><a href="https://crossfirefps.fandom.com/wiki/AWM"> AWM Sniper</a></h3>
<img src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/crossfirefps/images/d/d3/Sniper_AWM.png/revision/latest?cb=20190610234105"width="350">
<p>The AWM is a powerful sniper rifle that can eliminate enemies with one shot. It is commonly used for long-range combat.</p>

<h3><a href="https://crossfirefps.fandom.com/wiki/Desert_Eagle"> Desert Eagle</a></h3>
<img src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/crossfirefps/images/0/05/Pistol_DesertEagle.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width/360?cb=20190611001521">
<p>The Desert Eagle is a strong handgun with high damage. It is often used as a secondary weapon when players run out of rifle ammo.</p>
